Intensive Behavioral Therapy (IBT)

Willpower runs out; habit engineering doesn't. Every Vitality patient gets our structured IBT protocol that rewires triggers, routines, and rewards.

  • ›Spot emotional and situational eating triggers before they become lapses
  • ›Build micro-habits that compound into sustainable lifestyle change
  • ›Cognitive restructuring around food, body image, and health identity
  • ›Relapse prevention with ongoing accountability touchpoints

Personal counseling & education

Weight loss without education is temporary; with understanding it's permanent.

  • ›Sustainability education: hold progress through travel, stress, holidays, and life transitions
  • ›Biomarker literacy: your InBody numbers in plain language so every re-scan motivates
  • ›Identity-level change: from "I'm on a diet" to "This is how I live now"

Registered Dietitian guidance

Your nutrition plan isn't pulled from a template. A Registered Dietitian calibrates it against your InBody composition, body-fat percentage, lean mass, and your goals.

  • ›Body-fat-driven targeting: macros tuned to your current and target body-fat, not a one-size number
  • ›Lean-mass protection: protein floors and resistance fueling to keep muscle while fat comes off
  • ›Goal-specific plans: fat loss, recomposition, performance, post-bariatric, or metabolic-first
  • ›Condition-aware: GLP-1 side effects, insulin resistance, PCOS, thyroid, GI sensitivities
  • ›Ongoing re-calibration: every InBody re-scan triggers an RD review

Your personal fitness coordinator

You're not handed a workout sheet and sent away. A dedicated coordinator trains you hands-on so you can confidently run your plan on your own.

  • ›One-on-one training: walked through every movement until it's second nature
  • ›Technique mastery: correct form, tempo, and safety cues so you avoid injury
  • ›Gym autonomy: independence, not dependency, so you can train at any gym
  • ›Plan personalization: chosen around your capacity, injuries, and equipment
  • ›Facility onboarding: come in, learn your program, leave with a clear path
